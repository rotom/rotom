Thomas de Haven Roberts

I am a postdoc at the Institute for Logic, Language, and Computation at the University of Amsterdam. Before that I did my PhD in Linguistics at UC Santa Cruz.

As a semantico-pragmagician, I construct formal mathematical models of natural language meaning and real-world conversational context. Some perennial obsessions include clause-embedding, negation, tense/aspect, discourse particles, the interpretation of biased questions, and pragmatic and ethical questions concerning internet speech.
